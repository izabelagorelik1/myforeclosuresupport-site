Common Questions About Foreclosure Help

Honest, plain-English answers to the most common questions homeowners ask when facing foreclosure.

Can I stop foreclosure after receiving a notice?

Yes — depending on your lender and state laws, you may still have legal rights to delay or stop the process. Our team can guide you through options like reinstatement, repayment plans, loan modification, or selling before auction.

Do you charge for consultations?

No. All consultations through MyForeclosureSupport.com are 100% free and confidential. You’ll speak with a real person who explains your options clearly and without judgment.

Can I still get help if the foreclosure sale date is soon?

Yes, even if your sale date is approaching, there are emergency options such as last-minute reinstatement, short-term refinancing, or negotiation with your lender. Contact us right away for guidance.

Is this available outside New Jersey?

Yes! We assist homeowners in multiple states including North Carolina, South Carolina, and Georgia — and can refer trusted partners in others.

What happens after I submit the form?

Once you send your form, our team reviews your situation within one business day. We’ll reach out by phone or email to discuss your best options in simple, plain language.
